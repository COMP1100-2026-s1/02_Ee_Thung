# 🗺️ Business Model Canvas
![alt text](<BMC_COMP1100 (2).png>)

## 👥 Customer Segments
- **Socially Anxious Domestic Students** — Local students who want to participate but are prevented by the emotional "first-meeting" hurdle and fear of attending alone
- **Connection Seeking International Students** — Students looking for "home-away-from-home" communities and low-friction social entry points
- **New UQ Students** — First-year students overwhelmed by the 220+ UQ clubs who need a guided, activity-first path to engagement

## 💡 Value Propositions
- **Skill-Matched Social Entry* — We provide "psychological safety" through Tiered Open Socials. Unlike generic club lists, we guarantee students will match with peers at their exact goal/tier level (Newbie/Hobbyist/Competitive), eliminating the fear of "not being good enough."
- **Immediacy-Based Discovery** — While existing UQ sites are static encyclopedias of clubs, we provide a Live Activity Feed. We solve the "Information Fragmentation" problem by focusing on what is happening right now (e.g., social tennis in 30 minutes) rather than requiring students to join a club weeks in advance.
- **Human-to-Human Pathfinding** — For the most isolated students, we offer 1-on-1 Personalized Onboarding. We provide a human bridge (mentors) to physically walk students into their first activity, a high-touch service that automated directories cannot offer.

## 🤝 Key Partners
- **UQ Union (UQU)** — Crucial for official club data, legitimacy, and integrating our platform as the "Live" extension of the club directory.
- **Club Executives** — Partners who provide real-time event "vibes" and schedules to keep the activity feed accurate.
- **UQ Sport & UQ Life** — To integrate facility bookings (e.g., courts/rooms) directly into the "Open Social" creation flow.
- **Faculty Societies** — To provide the "Social Anchors" (senior mentors) for the 1-on-1 Pathfinder sessions.

## ⚙️ Key Activities
- **Live Data Aggregation** — Turning fragmented club announcements into a single, real-time "Source of Truth" feed.
- **Skill-Tier Verification** — Managing the community rating system to ensure Open Socials remain fair, safe, and accurately leveled.
- **Pathfinder Matching** — Facilitating the 1-on-1 connections between isolated new students and senior student mentors.

## 🛠️ Key Resources
- **The "Skill-Level" Database** — Proprietary data on student capabilities and interests that allows for personalized matching.
- **The Pathfinder Network** — A trained group of senior students who serve as the human "social anchors" for the platform.
- **Digital Infrastructure** — The recommendation engine that maps student timetables to available Open Socials.

## 💬 Customer Relationships
- **Peer-to-Peer Community (Open Socials)** — A community-driven model where students create their own value by hosting activities for others.
- **High-Touch "Pathfinding"** — Deep, personal relationships through 1-on-1 consultations for the most vulnerable/isolated segments.
- **Automated Personalization** — Algorithmic matching based on "Career vs. Wellness" goals.
- **On the Ground Support** — Physical presence and direct assistance at events, with Pathfinder mentors providing real-time guidance to help new students navigate their first activity experience.

## 📣 Channels
- **Market Day** — Physical outreach to build trust and demonstrate the app's "Open Social" feature in real-time.
- **Digital Discovery Platform** — Targeted reach via Discord and Instagram to capture students where they are already searching.
- **Lecture/Tutorial Shilling** — Direct reach into high-density courses to lower the "Discovery Barrier" for time-poor students.

## 💸 Cost Structure
- **Platform R&D & Hosting** — Costs for maintaining the live data feed and recommendation algorithms.
- **Operations & Events** — Operational costs for the senior students facilitating the human-bridge sessions.
- **Marketing & Acquisition** — Physical and digital costs for student acquisition.
- **Customer Support & Moderation** — Costs for managing community interactions and ensuring safe, inclusive spaces across all Open Socials and platform channels.

## 💰 Revenue Streams
- **Institutional Partnerships** — Revenue from UQU or UQ Life for measurable increases in student retention and mental wellness.
- **Transaction/Booking Fees** — Small processing fees for "Open Social" activities or high-demand event tickets.
- **Club Marketing Slots & Membership Bridging** — Premium "Featured" slots for clubs to boost event visibility, plus commission/transaction fees from membership purchases when users buy club memberships through our app and bridge to external platforms (e.g., Humanitrix).
