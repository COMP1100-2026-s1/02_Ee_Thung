# 🗺️ Business Model Canvas
![alt text](<BMC_COMP1100 (2).png>)

## 👥 Customer Segments
- **Socially Anxious Domestic Students** — Fear of attending alone; blocked by "first-meeting" hurdle
- **Connection-Seeking International Students** — Need low-friction entry to social communities
- **New UQ Students** — Overwhelmed by 220+ clubs; need activity-first guidance

✅ **Accepted** (LC4, LC8, LC9): 80% of students confirmed social anxiety as the primary barrier to joining clubs.
❌ **Refuted** (LC3): Original assumption that international students prioritise social connection while domestic students prioritise career was too binary — both groups seek a balance of social and career activities.

## 💡 Value Propositions
- **Skill-Matched Social Entry** — Tiered Open Socials match students by level (Newbie/Hobbyist/Competitive); no fear of "not being good enough"
- **Immediacy-Based Discovery** — Live Activity Feed shows events happening now; no advance sign-up required
- **Human-to-Human Pathfinding** — Mentors physically walk isolated students into their first activity

✅ **Accepted** (LC1, LC5, LC6): Centralised real-time discovery validated by 80% of students. Category-based filtering confirmed as MVP anchor.
✅ **Accepted with pivot** (LC8, LC9): Pathfinder concept accepted but formal "consultation" framing refuted — reframed to casual "Book a Chat" with rich mentor profiles and pre-filled icebreakers.

## 🤝 Key Partners
- **UQ Union (UQU)** — Official club data + platform legitimacy
- **Club Executives** — Real-time event schedules + activity "vibes"
- **UQ Sport & UQ Life** — Facility bookings integrated into Open Social flow
- **Faculty Societies** — Senior mentors for 1-on-1 Pathfinder sessions

✅ **Accepted** (LC10, LC11): Official UQ endorsement and SSO integration confirmed as critical to overcoming platform migration hesitancy. Club execs validated as key partners but require multi-admin tools and cross-posting support.

## ⚙️ Key Activities
- **Live Data Aggregation** — One real-time feed from fragmented club announcements
- **Skill-Tier Verification** — Community rating system keeps Open Socials accurately levelled
- **Pathfinder Matching** — Pairs isolated students with senior mentors
- **Cross-Posting to Existing Platforms** *(new)* — Automated sync to Discord/Facebook/Instagram

✅ **Accepted** (LC1, LC11): Live aggregation validated as core need. Cross-posting added as critical activity after 90% of club execs flagged redundant posting as a dealbreaker.
✅ **Accepted** (LC9): Pathfinder matching expanded to include rich profile display and pre-written icebreakers.

## 🛠️ Key Resources
- **Skill-Level Database** — Student capability + interest data for personalised matching
- **Pathfinder Network** — Trained senior students as "social anchors"
- **Digital Infrastructure** — Recommendation engine mapping timetables to Open Socials
- **UQ SSO Integration** *(new)* — Single sign-on for trust and legitimacy

✅ **Accepted** (LC12): 70% of students discovered niche clubs they wouldn't have otherwise found. Personalised "For Your Page" feed flagged as high-priority future resource.
✅ **Accepted** (LC10): UQ SSO identified as critical resource for overcoming platform migration friction.

## 💬 Customer Relationships
- **Peer-to-Peer Community** — Students host Open Socials for each other
- **High-Touch Pathfinding** — 1-on-1 sessions for most isolated students
- **Automated Personalisation** — Matching based on Career vs. Wellness goals
- **On-the-Ground Support** — Mentors present at events for real-time guidance

✅ **Accepted** (LC3, LC9): Dual career/wellness categorisation validated. High-touch Pathfinding accepted with low-stakes casual framing and rich mentor profiles.
❌ **Refuted** (LC8): Formal "consultation" style booking refuted — perceived as gatekeeping. Replaced with casual peer chat model.

## 📣 Channels
- **Market Day** — Live Open Social demo; in-person trust building
- **Discord & Instagram** — Targeted reach where students already are
- **Lecture/Tutorial Outreach** — Direct access to high-density courses

✅ **Accepted** (LC2): Market Day validated for impulse discovery but insufficient alone. Digital channels essential for planned discovery.
✅ **Accepted** (LC10): Discord and existing platforms should be linked rather than replaced — UQ-Connect positions as a gateway, not a replacement.

## 💸 Cost Structure
- **Platform R&D & Hosting** — Live feed + recommendation algorithm
- **Operations & Events** — Mentor-facilitated sessions
- **Marketing & Acquisition** — Physical + digital outreach
- **Support & Moderation** — Community safety across all channels
- **Cross-Platform API Integration** *(new)* — Dev costs for Discord/Facebook/Instagram sync

⚠️ **Expanded** (LC11, LC13): Additional dev costs anticipated for multi-admin co-management, RSVP tracking, attendee capacity limits, ticket link integration, and campus map integration.

## 💰 Revenue Streams
- **Institutional Partnerships** — UQU/UQ Life fees tied to retention + wellness outcomes
- **Transaction/Booking Fees** — Small fees on Open Socials + high-demand events
- **Club Marketing & Membership Bridging** — Featured club slots + commission on in-app membership purchases (e.g., Humanitrix)

✅ **Accepted** (LC10): Institutional partnership model strengthened — UQ endorsement is both a revenue and legitimacy lever.
✅ **Accepted** (LC13): Transaction/booking fee stream validated — students and execs flagged payment/ticketing integration as a needed feature for paid events.
